Services We Offer

Empowering Safety Solutions for All

CCTV Installation & Maintenance

We provide complete CCTV system setup for homes, offices, and public facilities. Our team ensures proper installation, reliable coverage, and long-term maintenance to keep your property secure 24/7.

Smart Security System Integration

Upgrade your security with smart alarms, motion detectors, and mobile-enabled systems. We help you control and monitor your property remotely with modern, easy-to-use technology.

IT & Network Setup

We design and install stable network systems to support CCTV, alarms, and other tech solutions. From Wi-Fi setups to structured cabling, we ensure smooth and secure connectivity.

Technical Support & User Training

Our support doesn’t end after installation. We offer user training, technical assistance, and repairs to ensure our clients get the most out of their systems.

AI-Powered Surveillance with Facial Recognition

Our advanced security cameras use AI to detect faces, monitor movement, and alert officials in real-time — ideal for barangay checkpoints, entrances, and public areas.

Emergency Alert Systems for Communities

Our systems can send real-time alerts for emergencies like theft, fire, or accidents — helping barangay officials respond faster.
